Case study:

Alffie’s website

I saw a problem

If you were to go to Alffie’s website, you'd likely have a hard time working out what they do and how they do it.


I created a prototype website

I put together a proposal to demonstrate the benefits and value that good interface design can add to an organisation. I used Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and XD to show what an end product may look like, then coded a prototype website using HTML, CSS and Javascript.


Alffie’s website is currently a large mix of styles and colours, and there is no central theme or message. For my design, I drastically scaled back the amount of colours used, basing everything on a style guide that I created. I also developed some ideas around the style of copy I wanted. There should be strong calls to action and Alffie should quickly identify who they are, the problem they solve and how they do it.
